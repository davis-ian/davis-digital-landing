# Davis Digital Business Brief

## Business Overview

Davis Digital is a digital services studio focused on helping small businesses simplify their technology, launch polished web experiences, and keep their online presence working after launch.

Core promise: Simplify the tech. Stunning results. Seamless growth.

Primary services:

- Web development
- Monthly website maintenance
- Search engine optimization
- Social media management

## Positioning

Davis Digital should be positioned as a reliable technical and creative partner for businesses that need modern digital infrastructure without needing to manage the technical details themselves.

The brand should communicate:

- Clear, jargon-free technical help
- Professional execution without bloated agency complexity
- Practical growth support across websites, SEO, maintenance, and social channels
- A long-term partner mindset, not just a one-time website vendor

## Target Customers

Best-fit customers include:

- Small businesses that need a professional website launch or refresh
- Service businesses that need a clearer online presence and lead flow
- Founders or operators who do not want to manage website maintenance themselves
- Businesses with existing websites that need SEO, speed, technical cleanup, or growth support
- Local or regional brands that need consistent social media management
- Growing businesses that need custom web functionality such as payments, dashboards, user accounts, or API integrations

## Brand Voice

The voice should be confident, direct, and plainspoken.

Use phrases that emphasize outcomes:

- Built to launch cleanly and grow with you
- We handle the tech so you can run the business
- Clear pricing, practical strategy, polished execution
- Protect your investment after launch
- Stop chasing traffic. Start driving revenue.

Avoid:

- Heavy technical jargon without a business benefit
- Generic agency language like "innovative solutions" or "digital transformation"
- Overpromising guaranteed rankings, revenue, or viral growth

## Visual Branding Notes

The pricing sheets establish a strong visual direction:

- High-contrast black and white foundation
- Rounded white label shapes on dark backgrounds
- Monospaced or technical display typography for section headings
- Clean sans-serif body copy for readability
- Minimal color palette with a possible purple accent for emphasis, borders, or active states
- Simple cards with bold package names and clear pricing
- Strong spacing, large type, and confident contrast

The website should preserve the existing brand feel while improving readability and conversion. Use the bold black-and-white visual language for hero sections, service labels, and major calls to action. Use lighter sections where dense pricing details need to be easier to scan.

## Conversion Strategy

Primary conversion goal:

- Get qualified prospects to start a project conversation or request a scope.

Secondary conversion goals:

- Help visitors quickly self-select the right service category
- Use visible starting prices to qualify leads
- Build confidence through process clarity, launch timelines, and support expectations
- Position maintenance and SEO as natural next steps after a web build

Recommended CTAs:

- Start a Project
- Request a Scope
- View Services
- Book a Consultation
- Not sure what you need? Let's talk.

Conversion principles:

- Keep pricing visible but framed as starting points
- Put the strongest CTA above the fold
- Repeat a CTA after the services overview and after pricing sections
- Use short proof-oriented bullets near CTAs: mobile-friendly, launch timeline, walkthrough hand-off, priority support
- Avoid overwhelming the homepage with every package detail; send detailed comparisons to the services page

## Website Copy Plan

### Homepage

Goal: Explain who Davis Digital helps, what services are offered, and why the visitor should start a project conversation.

Recommended sections:

- Hero with core promise and primary CTA
- Services overview with four cards
- Featured pricing highlights
- Why Davis Digital
- Process overview
- Final conversion CTA

Homepage copy angle:

"Websites, SEO, maintenance, and social content built to help your business grow without managing the tech."

### Services Page

Goal: Explain the four service categories without overwhelming visitors or forcing them into a rigid public price grid.

Recommended sections:

- Services intro
- Web development overview with starter pricing only
- Monthly maintenance overview without full tier detail
- SEO overview scoped after review
- Social media overview with starting price only
- Scope request CTA

### Process Content

Goal: Keep process content short and embedded on Home or Services until there is enough proof for a dedicated Work/Case Studies page.

Recommended sections:

- Discover the goal
- Scope the right starting point
- Build, launch, and support the work
- CTA to contact

### Contact Page

Goal: Turn the page into a project inquiry entry point.

Recommended sections:

- Clear project conversation headline
- Good-fit services list
- Intake prompts: service needed, current website, budget, timeline, goals
- Email or booking CTA
- Reassurance around scoping and next steps
